Activities Offered:

There are many different things that you can do while on your trip.

You can choose between:

  • Swimming
  • Eating
  • Swimming and eating
  • Building sandcastles
  • Destroying sandcastles
  • Building a snowman (or sandman)
  • Climbing a tree maybe (to get to the treehouse)
  • Doing nothing

Activities vary depending on the season and location.
